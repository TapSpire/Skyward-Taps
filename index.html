🎮 HOW TO PLAY: Tapspire!

  • A word appears every 5 seconds. Decide fast!
  • Tap/click the word if it's spelled correctly.
  • ✔️ Gain $1 for a correct word, ❌ lose $1 for a misspelled one.
  • 🎲 Tap a tile on the grid to gamble its value.
  • If it’s an 8-tile, you win or lose an extra $8 based on the word!
  • 🚫 Red tiles can't be reused.
  • ⏱️ Bonus Time at scores: $15 (+15s), $30 (+30s), $60 (+60s)
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Don’t trust autocorrect. Trust yourself.
