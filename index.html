Tapspire!

Score: 0
Time: 30s

HOW TO PLAY:

  • A word will appear every 5 seconds.
  • Tap or click the word if it is correctly spelled
  • $1 gained for each correct word and $1 lost for each incorrect word
  • You have the option of gambling by clicking on the grid
  • If you last clicked the tile with 8, you will either gain or lose $8
  • This is in addition to the $1 gained or lost
  • Once the value in the tile becomes red, you cannot reuse that tile
  • If you reach a score of $15, you get a 15-second time bonus
  • For 30 points, you get 30 seconds, and for 60 points, 60 seconds
